╔══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║                         TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE                                ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

  Problem: "File not found" errors
  Solution: Set Root Directory to "shl-agent"

  Problem: "GROQ_API_KEY not found"
  Solution: Add GROQ_API_KEY in Environment tab

  Problem: Build fails with "metadata-generation-failed"
  Solution: Ensure Build Command is "bash render-build.sh"

  Problem: Service shows 503 error
  Solution: Wait 10-15 seconds, check logs, verify GROQ_API_KEY

  Problem: Service spins down after 15 minutes
  Solution: This is normal on free tier. Upgrade to Starter ($7/mo) for always-on
